Capti Voice

Capti Voice is a reading support tool for education, work, and entertainment. It can be used by K-12 schools, colleges, businesses, and individuals. Choose your role above to learn more.

Capti makes it easy to create playlists with textual content to have them narrated by high quality text-to-speech in over 20 languages, improve vocabulary, and develop active reading strategies.

Capti offers easy access to OCR for digitizing PDFs and images to make them accessible, active reading tools, contextually-aware reference tools for vocabulary building and more.

Capti can be used with any content, including Bookshare, Web pages, printed media, Google Drive, OneDrive, etc. across a variety of devices anywhere and anytime.

Capti Voice is universally accessible in that it works across user devices, content sources, content formats, individual abilities, age groups, instructional goals, and subject areas.
