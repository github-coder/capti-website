Where is Capti useful

Capti is useful in many scenarios. For example, you can add morning news to your Playlist and start listening to it on your iPad at home, continue listening from the same spot on the iPhone during your commute, add some documents to your Playlist online when you are at work or at school; catch up with the reading while you are exercising at the gym; and relax on the couch at home while listening to your computer narrating your favorite e-book.

Capti will synchronize your Playlist among all your devices, making the transitions seamless. Importantly, Capti stores your documents on your device, so you don’t need internet access to listen to them.
